# Recent Developments: Skills
**Last updated:** 2026-03-20

## Current Landscape
The skills ecosystem has grown rapidly since Skills 2.0 launched. The SKILL.md format is now an industry-wide open standard, a community marketplace lists 65,000+ skills, and adoption has expanded from developers into executive and non-technical use cases.

## Entries

### Skills at Scale (Uber Livestream)
- **What:** Thariq hosted a livestream with an Uber engineer demonstrating skills in large engineering teams. Uber open-sourced an iMessage skill as a real-world example.
- **Why it matters:** Validates skills as a serious tool for enterprise teams, not just solo developers. Key quote: "Using skills well is a skill issue" -- the best skills transform entire team workflows.
- **Added:** 2026-03-20

### YC CEO Skill Pack (Garry Tan)
- **What:** Y Combinator CEO Garry Tan released 8 skills including `/plan-ceo-review` for founder-perspective analysis and strategic planning.
- **Why it matters:** Signals executive-level adoption. Skills are no longer just for writing code -- they're being used for business strategy and decision-making.
- **Added:** 2026-03-20

### DTC Brand Skills (Mike Futia)
- **What:** A skills pack for direct-to-consumer brands covering competitor audits, creative briefs, hook variations, ad copy, static ad generation (Nano Banana 2), influencer outreach, and Google Workspace CLI.
- **Why it matters:** Shows skills expanding into marketing and brand management. Practical example of niche packaging -- bundling related workflows for a specific industry.
- **Added:** 2026-03-20

### 65K+ Skills Marketplace
- **What:** Community-driven directory listing over 65,000 skills across many categories and niches.
- **Why it matters:** The ecosystem has reached critical mass. Most common workflows likely already have a skill you can use or adapt.
- **Added:** 2026-03-20

### Excel and PowerPoint Add-ins
- **What:** Skills are available in Microsoft Office add-ins. Teams save workflows as skills; others run them with one click from the Office sidebar.
- **Why it matters:** Brings skills to non-technical users who never touch a command line. Dramatically widens who can benefit from skills.
- **Added:** 2026-03-20

### Google DESIGN.md (Stitch AI)
- **What:** Google shipped a DESIGN.md file in their Stitch AI design tool, mirroring the structured markdown instruction file concept.
- **Why it matters:** Confirms the pattern is spreading industry-wide. Structured .md instruction files are becoming a standard way to give AI tools reusable instructions, regardless of vendor.
- **Added:** 2026-03-20

### Skill Sync Pain Point
- **What:** Keeping skills in sync between Claude Chat, Claude Code, and across team members has no native solution yet.
- **Why it matters:** A known gap in the ecosystem. The community is actively requesting a sync mechanism. Worth watching for updates.
- **Added:** 2026-03-20
